Frequently Asked Questions


How much does it cost to join the society?
It only costs £1 which can be given to any of the committee and this will let you take part in all the events that take place throughout the year.
Where is the society based?
Most events take place in the Queen Mother Building right next to the union as this is the building for computing. We also have other events which take place all around dundee.
Where and when can I see the committee members of the society?
There’s almost always a committee member in the Queen Mother Building in the day, usually in one of the labs.
