About Us

Welcome to the Dundee University Computing Society (DUCS) website. This society was created for computing students to meet, socialise and learn a bit about computing at our events which take place all throughout the year. Join the society and you will be able to attend events such as the annual ball, tech meetups and our 24 hour hackathons which is sponsored by companies, giving you more opportunities in the computing industry.


DUCS constantly have events happening throughout the year for students to participate in to learn more about the technology industry or just to meet new people. Past events that have taken place this past couple of months are the pub crawl, the pool night that took place in the union and most notably the Quackathon. Dundee is famous for its games industry (GTA, Lemmings, Minecraft and Crackdown), the society has been very popular among students which has helped keep DUCS running for a few years now.
